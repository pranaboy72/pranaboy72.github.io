Junwoo Chang

I am currently a M.S. student at Machine Learning and Control Systems Lab (MLCS) at Yonsei University, advised by Prof. Jongeun Choi. My research interests lie at the intersection of robotics and machine learning, focusing on symmetry-aware reinforcement learning for improved sample efficiency and generalization.
